The current (editors draft) ODRL Ontology and can be downloaded in Turtle serialisation.

Other serialisations (RDF/XML and N-Triples) will be provided when the ODRL Ontology is finalised.

Target Asset

The use of other ontology predicates to associate Assets with Policies MAY be supported using the RDF/OWL encoding. In these cases, the ODRL Policy MUST not define the target Asset, as this will be inferred from the axiom.

Consider the below example; the Dublin Core [[dcterms]] license predicate is used to infer the relationship between the Asset and the Policy. This axiom infers that the Asset <http://example.com/asset:9898> is the target Asset for the Policy <http://example.com/policy:0099>.

@prefix odrl: <http://www.w3.org/ns/odrl/2/> .
@prefix dct: <http://purl.org/dc/terms/> .

 <http://example.com/asset:9898> dct:license <http://example.com/policy:0099> .

 <http://example.com/policy:0099>
	  a odrl:Set;
	  odrl:permission odrl:reproduce ;
	  odrl:prohibition odrl:modify .
